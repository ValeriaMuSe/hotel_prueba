La Kukula Lodge

About us

La Kukula Lodge is located in Puerto Viejo, just 200 meters from Chiquita Beach. It offers free Wi-Fi and an outdoor pool. Rooms at this tropical lodge feature a terrace with jungle views and a private bathroom with a rain shower. They also include bed linen, a safe and a fan. La Kukula Lodge has a garden, bar, laundry service and a tour desk. Free parking is provided. Punta Uva beach and Jaguar Wild Life jaguar rescue center are 1.5 km away
