Manzanillo

About Manzanillo

This area is located in the Caribbean part of Costa Rica and is the last town before reaching Panama. Beach with palm trees and white sand ideal for diving and watching dolphins, with manatees in the surrounding mangroves and together with its inhabitants with their Afro-Caribbean culture with unique characteristics not very visible in the rest of CR, such as their speech, which is a mixture of English and Spanish called patua, reggae music, and unpaved streets is the synonym of no stress.
